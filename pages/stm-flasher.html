STM32 Flashing Requirements

  • STM32 board in DFU mode (press BOOT button, reset, release BOOT)
  • USB connection to your computer
  • Chrome/Edge browser with WebSerial or WebUSB support
  • HTTPS connection (ar3s-compiler.duckdns.org)

Flash firmware to STM32 boards using WebSerial/WebUSB. Direct firmware deployment from your browser.
